SUITES

These 4 Colonial Suites are the ultimate in colonial comfort and luxury making them exclusive and always in high demand. They come with a separate sitting area with an additional television, and a dining table. The huge wooden four poster and canopied beds designed in traditional colonial style lend the suites an authentic colonial aura. The luxurious feel of these suites are further enhanced by the antique wooden furniture and wood flooring that whisper secrets of intrigue and adventure. We have not spared any modern amenity; every facility from satellite TV, a digital safe and a Wi-Fi to International Direct Dialling (IDD) are available. Amenities have been tastefully blended with the decor. The comfort and luxury of these suites extend to the spacious bathrooms that are fitted with luxurious bathtubs. The suite bathrooms come fully supplied. Feel like a guest of the Governor in one of the premier colonial hotels in US, as you enjoy the laid-back lifestyle of the colonial era.

Room Pricing and Customization

At our hotel, we offer a variety of room types and suites, with prices that may vary depending on the customer's preferences and selected facilities. Our room rates are adjusted based on the view (city or lake facing), bed type (king or twin), and any additional services such as spa access, gourmet dining, or private pools. For example, our Deluxe Rooms start from $350, with higher rates for lake-facing views or enhanced amenities like in-room dining and wellness services. Similarly, our exclusive Tata Suite offers unparalleled luxury starting from $25,000 per night, with options to customize your stay with private gyms, bulletproof glass, and personalized concierge services. Each stay is crafted to meet your unique needs, ensuring that every guest enjoys a tailored and unforgettable experience.
