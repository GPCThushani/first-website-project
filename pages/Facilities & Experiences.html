UNWIND IN LUXURY

World-Class Amenities at Your Fingertips

At our hotel, we strive to offer more than just a place to stay-we provide a sanctuary where comfort, convenience, and indulgence meet. Whether you're here for business or pleasure, our carefully curated facilities ensure that every aspect of your visit is effortless and enjoyable. From the tranquility of our luxurious spa to the exquisite flavors of our on-site restaurants, we've thoughtfully designed our spaces to elevate your experience. Enjoy premium accommodations, relax by the pool, stay connected with our business services, and let our attentive staff take care of the rest. Your perfect stay awaits.

Accommodation Features
Our elegantly designed rooms and suites offer a blend of comfort and style,featuring modern amenities like free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, minibars, and premium bedding for a truly relaxing stay.

Dining Options
Savor a diverse selection of gourmet dishes at our on-site restaurants, offering international and local cuisine. Enjoy a relaxing atmosphere at our bars and lounges, perfect for unwinding with your favorite drinks.

Spa & Wellness
Indulge in ultimate relaxation at our luxurious spa, offering rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials. Stay fit in our fully-equipped fitness center, or unwind in our sauna and steam rooms.

Swimming Pool
 Take a dip in our outdoor/indoor swimming pool, perfect for a refreshing swim or a relaxing day by the poolside. Poolside service is available for drinks and snacks.

Meeting and Event Spaces
 Our versatile meeting rooms and event spaces are ideal for conferences, weddings, and private gatherings. Equipped with modern technology and professional event planning services.

Business Services
 Our 24-hour business center offers all the essential services for the business traveler, including computers, printers, and high-speed internet access.

`

Recreational Facilities
 Enjoy a variety of recreational activities, including tennis courts, a fitness center, and a kids' club to keep younger guests entertained.

Transportation and Parking
 We offer convenient on-site parking and a complimentary shuttle service to the airport or local attractions, making travel hassle-free for our guests.

Concierge Services
 Our knowledgeable concierge is available to assist with arranging tours, restaurant reservations, and tickets to local attractions, ensuring you make the most of your stay.

Pet-Friendly Amenities
 We welcome your four-legged friends with pet-friendly rooms and amenities like pet beds and bowls, ensuring they enjoy their stay as much as you do.

Accessibility Features
 Our hotel is equipped with accessible rooms and facilities, including ramps, elevators, and accessible bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.

Sustainability and Green Initiatives
 We are committed to sustainable practices, including energy-saving initiatives, waste reduction, and eco-friendly services, helping to protect the environment while you enjoy your stay.

Private Resort Amenities
 Guests can enjoy exclusive access to our private resort, complete with lounge chairs, umbrellas, and riverside service for a luxurious riverside experience.

Laundry and Dry-Cleaning Service
 For your convenience, we offer same-day laundry and dry-cleaning services, ensuring you always look your best during your stay.

Currency Exchange and ATM
 For easy access to local currency, we provide currency exchange services and an on-site ATM available 24/7.

UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCES AT GOURMET GATEWAY

Gourmet Gateway is the epicentre of refinement and personalised service in Portland. Discover why staying with us is one of the most delightful experiences in Portland.

Centrally located in Portland's business district, It faces the historic Village Face Green. With lovely views of the Vancouver Lake, we are close to shopping, nightlife, cultural and historical landmarks. But our favourite among experiences in Portland is a stroll on the amazing river at our doorstep.
Collect memorable experiences during your stay.

Local Tours and Excursions

Discover the rich history and vibrant culture of the region through our curated local tours. Whether you're exploring historic landmarks or scenic landscapes, our guided excursions offer an immersive experience into the local life. Let our expert guides take you on a journey through the area's most iconic and off-the-beaten-path locations.

Culinary Experiences

Indulge in a gastronomic adventure with our exclusive culinary experiences. Join a local food tour to sample authentic regional dishes, or take part in hands-on cooking classes where you'll learn to prepare traditional recipes using fresh, local ingredients. Elevate your palate as you explore the flavors and techniques that define our cuisine.

Cultural Performances

Immerse yourself in the vibrant cultural heritage of the region through live performances right at the hotel or nearby venues. Enjoy traditional dance, music, and theatre that showcase the artistry and customs passed down through generations. Each performance offers a unique glimpse into the heart and soul of the local culture.

Adventure and Outdoor Activities

For thrill-seekers and nature lovers, we offer a variety of outdoor adventures, from hiking through scenic trails to water sports along the coast. Experience the excitement of kayaking, rafting, or even hot air balloon rides over stunning landscapes. Our team can arrange activities that cater to all levels of adventure, ensuring an unforgettable outdoor experience.

Wellness and Spa Retreats

Rejuvenate your mind, body, and spirit with our personalized wellness experiences. Enjoy holistic treatments at our world-class spa, or join a yoga session on the lake to reconnect with nature. Our wellness retreats are designed to help you find balance and leave you feeling refreshed and revitalized.

Romantic Experiences

Create unforgettable memories with our tailored romantic experiences. From candlelit dinners under the stars to private beach picnics, we offer a range of intimate moments for couples to enjoy. Let us arrange a special day or evening that celebrates love, whether it's for an anniversary, honeymoon, or simply to treat your partner.

Family-Friendly Adventures

Make lasting memories with your loved ones through our family-friendly experiences. From interactive kids' clubs to family excursions, we ensure that guests of all ages are entertained and engaged. Enjoy games, treasure hunts, and educational tours designed to provide fun and learning for the entire family.

Wellness Retreats and Mindfulness

Escape the hustle and bustle of daily life with our exclusive wellness retreats. Focus on your well-being with a range of mindfulness activities, including meditation, yoga, and detox treatments. Our peaceful surroundings and expert wellness staff will guide you towards relaxation and inner peace.

Water Sports and Activities

Take advantage of our prime river front location by diving into thrilling water sports and activities. Whether you're into jet skiing, paddleboarding, or simply relaxing with a cocktail by the shore, there's something for every type of water lover. Let our team assist in arranging the perfect aquatic adventure.

Art and Photography Tours

Discover the beauty of our location through the lens of art and photography. Join guided photography tours that take you to picturesque spots, or explore local galleries and exhibitions showcasing the work of regional artists. Whether you're an aspiring photographer or an art lover, these experiences will inspire creativity.
