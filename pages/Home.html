Welcome to Senses Spa

Portland's authentic Oriental spa experience. Let our Balinese and Thai therapists help re-align and balance your energies during your stay at The Gourmet Gateway. Experience a rejuvenating pampering session with a range of treatments offered here in a premium, luxury spa in Portland.

Embark on a wellness journey infused with ancient healing energies and experiences, revitalizing your mind, body,...

Here are the massage and treatment that you can experiencing from our Gourmet Gateway Hotel.

Signature Massage

Experience our exclusive Signature Massage, a unique blend of massage techniques customized to meet your specific needs. This treat combines elements of relaxation, deep tissue, and stretching to relieve tension and restore balance to both body and mind.

Body Massage

Indulge in a Body Massage designed to relax the entire body, release tension, and improve circulation. Whether you prefer a gentle, soothe touch or a deeper pressure, our therapists tailor the session to your preferences for a rejuvenating experience.

Ayurveda Massage

Rooted in ancient Indian traditions, our Ayurveda Massage uses warm herbal oils tailored to your body type (dosha) to balance energy, detoxify, and promote overall well-being. This holistic treatment nurtures both physical and emotional harmony.

Hot Stone Massage

Surrender to the warmth our Hot Stone Massage, where smooth, heated stones are placed on key points of the body to melt away ten sion and promote deep relaxation. The combination of heat and expert massage techniques provides a deeply soothing experience.

Oriental Massage

Drawing from Eastern practices, our Oriental Massage blends acupressure, stretching, and rhythmic movements to restore energy flow and relieve tension. This massage targets pressure points to promote holistic healing and relaxation.

Facial Treatments

Refresh and rejuvenate your skin with our luxurious Facial Treatments. Whether you're looking for hydration, anti-aging, or deep clean sing, our customized facials use high-quality products to nourish and revitalize your complexion.

Silk Body Scrub

Pamper your skin with our Body Scrub, an exfoliating treatment that removes dead skin cells, leaving your body feeling silky smooth and radiant. The nourishing ingredients promote skin renewal, leaving you with a healthy, glowing complexion.
