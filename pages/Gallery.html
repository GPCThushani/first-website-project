Photos & Videos of the Gourmet Gateway

Our spacious and elegant hotel in Lewis Center, Portland is located on 15-acres of village-like grounds, creating a secluded ambience ideal for your next event or vacation!

Spend a night in one of our hotel's 193 well-appointed guest rooms, ranging from large family-friendly suites to classic king rooms ideal for business travelers. Our large hotel features deluxe amenities like indoor and outdoor pools, a wellness center, and complimentary parking. Our hotel is situated next to Highbanks Metro Park, a stunning stretch of nature known for its 100 foot-high shale bluff overlooking the Vancouver Lake.

Our IACC certified conference center features 25 meeting and breakout rooms, perfectly customizable for any kind of professional event. Choose from one of our inclusive meeting packages and receive special meeting room amenities like an on-site technology support specialist and state-of-the-art, integrated technology.

Our beautiful property is ideal for a romantic wedding. Our hotel has hosted hundreds of elegant weddings, and our on-site professional event team works with
each couple to create the wedding of their dreams.
