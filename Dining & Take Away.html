Daycation Packages


Escape for a Day!

Treat yourself to a mini-vacation without the overnight stay! Our Daycation Package offers the perfect get away to unwind, relax, and indulge in luxury right at our hotel.
Whether you're looking to escape your daily routine or enjoy a special day with loved ones, our daycation experience is designed just for you.



What's Included
  • Access to Hotel Amenities:
    • Enjoy full access to our stunning outdoor pool and relaxing hot tub.
    • Unwind in our serene spa area, complete with sauna and steam rooms.
  • Dining Experience:
    • Savor a delightful lunch from our exclusive daycation menu at Gourmet Gateway.
    • Enjoy a complimentary drink (choose from a selection of cocktails, wines, or non-alcoholic beverages).
  • Activities and Wellness:
    • Participate in our morning yoga class on the rooftop terrace.
    • Join a cooking workshop or a mixology class with our expert chefs.
  • Relaxation Areas:
    • Access to private lounges and cozy cabanas by the pool.
    • Enjoy our lush gardens and outdoor seating areas.
Pricing
  • Individual Daycation: $99
  • Couples Daycation: $179
  • Family Daycation: $299 (up to 4 guests)
Prices are subject to availability and do not include taxes and service charges. Additional guests can be added for $50 each.

Timeframe

  • Check-in: 9:00 AM
  • Check-out: 6:00 PM

Flexible timing can be arranged upon request.

Terms and Conditions

  • Reservations are required and subject to availability.
  • Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance to avoid charges.
  • Guests must be 18 years or older to access the spa facilities.

Why Choose Our Daycation?

  • Affordability: Enjoy a luxurious day away from home without breaking the bank.
  • Convenience: Perfect for those busy schedules, our daycation fits seamlessly into your day.
  • Quality Time: Whether with friends, family, or that special someone, create unforgettable memories.
